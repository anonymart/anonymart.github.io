What is this?

We all love open source projects, but too often they die from lack of momentum. Anonymart bounties hopes to solve that problem by openly and transparently rewarding contributors. On the checkout page of every Anonymart will be a bounty option that looks like this.

Link

Every now and then each Anonymart storefront pulls down a list of completed bounties and randomly chooses an address based on the weighted sum of completed bounties. The winner is rewarded with the bounty.

If you want to suggest a bounty or think it should be re-valued, please reach out at http://reddit.com/r/anonymart.
